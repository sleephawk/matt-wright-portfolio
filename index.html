About Me

With dev competition high, I aim to bring a new spirit and perspective to the field. My story began in balancing creativity with mundanity: managing data, emails and staff by day, pouring energy into music & design passion projects by night.

After some soul searching, I aimed to bring my proficiency with systems and my love for innovative ideas into one career path. Somehow, software engineering became the clear answer. After coding solo for a year, I realised I was really enjoying myself, and took the next step to grow with the _nology programme.

Along with hard-earned technical skills, my soft skills have grown organically from an adventurous curiousity in early career life. My communication skills I attribute to pushing out of my comfort zone, with everything from a degree in Modern Drama to four months teaching in a rural village in Indonesia. I owe my adaptability to working in multiple fast paced administration roles, whilst freelancing and juggling a music career. My innovation shows through my dedication to bringing problem solving ideas to life, for example, making training animations for the archiving dept in London South Bank University, or bringing musical childrens' book readings to Bukit Lawang to encourage dynamic learning.

I am now dedicated to bringing this wealth of energy and experience to a software development role. Knowing myself, my aptitude for learning and future goals, I can't wait to start on this new chapter.
